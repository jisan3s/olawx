Software development agreement gone wrong

TechInnovate Inc., a growing software development company, entered into a $2.5 million contract with Global Solutions Corp. to develop a custom enterprise resource planning (ERP) system. The project was behind schedule due to numerous change requests from Global Solutions, and despite TechInnovate's efforts to accommodate these changes, Global Solutions terminated the contract and filed a lawsuit alleging breach of contract and seeking $5 million in damages.

TechInnovate came to Olawx facing potentially devastating financial consequences. The company's reputation was on the line, and the legal costs of defending against a well-funded opponent threatened to overwhelm their resources.

Key Legal Challenges
Contract Ambiguity

Multiple ambiguous clauses in the contract that could be interpreted differently

Documentation Gaps

Incomplete project documentation from our client's side

Resource Imbalance

Opposing party had significantly greater financial resources

Complex Technical Issues

Need to explain complex software development processes to the court

When TechInnovate came to us, they were facing a well-funded opponent with extensive legal resources. Global Solutions Corp. had already spent significant resources building their case and had a team of attorneys from a large national law firm. The contract documentation was extensive but poorly organized, with multiple change orders and email communications that needed to be analyzed. The original contract contained several ambiguous clauses that Global Solutions was attempting to interpret in their favor.

Additionally, TechInnovate's internal documentation was incomplete, making it challenging to reconstruct the timeline of events and demonstrate that Global Solutions' constant change requests were the primary cause of delays.

Our Legal Strategy

The multifaceted approach we developed to achieve success

01.

Comprehensive Document Review

We conducted an exhaustive review of all contract documents, change orders, and communications. Our team implemented a sophisticated e-discovery process to reconstruct the complete project timeline.

02.

Expert Witness Engagement

We engaged leading experts in software development and project management to analyze the project timeline, validate our client's approach, and testify about industry standards.

03.

Strategic Motion Practice

We filed a series of strategic motions that narrowed the issues in the case and limited the opponent's ability to pursue certain claims, reducing their leverage in negotiations.

04.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

We proposed mediation early in the process while simultaneously preparing for trial, demonstrating our willingness to resolve the matter efficiently while maintaining our readiness for litigation.

A Complete Victory

After 14 months of intense litigation, we achieved a complete victory for TechInnovate Inc. The court granted our motion for summary judgment on the key breach of contract claim, dismissing it with prejudice.

Rather than facing a $5 million liability, our client received an $850,000 settlement from Global Solutions Corp. to resolve outstanding invoices for work completed. This represented a net positive outcome of $5.85 million compared to the original claim against them.

Additionally, the court awarded attorneys' fees to TechInnovate, recognizing that Global Solutions had acted in bad faith in certain aspects of the litigation. The complete dismissal with prejudice prevents Global Solutions from bringing any further claims related to this matter.
